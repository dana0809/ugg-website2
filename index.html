About Us

Our story begins in the 1970s, with a surfer who broke convention and decided to live by his own authentic rules. After realizing life as an accountant was not for him, Brian Smith moved to California and recognized a desire to create sheepskin footwear. With a dream, Brian bravely pursued his passion, redefining his life and our culture forever. Now, we honor our origin story while living fully for today.
